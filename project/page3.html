Official trailer of Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a popular American sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s in the small town of Hawkins, Indiana. The story begins when a young boy, Will Byers, mysteriously disappears, and his friends, family, and the local police chief try to find him. During their search, they encounter a girl with supernatural powers, known as Eleven, who has escaped from a secret government laboratory. Together, the group uncovers a dark parallel dimension called the Upside Down, filled with terrifying creatures and dangerous mysteries. The series features strong friendships, family bonds, and bravery in the face of unknown horrors. Key characters include Mike Wheeler, Dustin Henderson, Lucas Sinclair, and Joyce Byers, each playing a vital role in confronting the strange and supernatural events in Hawkins. The show combines suspense, adventure, horror, and 80s nostalgia, making it thrilling for viewers of all ages.

Main Characters / Cast: Will Byers – Noah Schnapp, the boy who disappears.
