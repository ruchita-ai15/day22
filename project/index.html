This website is based on popular and engaging web series. Stranger Things presents a mix of science fiction, mystery, and strong friendship. All of Us Are Dead shows survival and emotions during a deadly zombie outbreak. The Railway Men is inspired by a real incident and highlights courage and humanity. Sweet Home explores fear and human desires through a world of terrifying monsters. Alice in Borderland focuses on survival through dangerous and intelligent games. Together, these series offer a perfect blend of action, thriller, horror, and storytelling.
