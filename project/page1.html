Official trailer of Alice in borderland

Main Story: Ryohei Arisu, a jobless and directionless young man, gets trapped in a deserted, dangerous version of Tokyo called the Borderland.
Survival Games: Arisu and his friends must play deadly survival games. Each game is represented by playing cards. Losing a game usually means death.
Main Characters / Cast: Ryohei Arisu – Played by Kento Yamazaki, the main protagonist. Yuzuha Usagi – Played by Tao Tsuchiya, a skilled climber and Arisu’s ally. Karube – Played by Keita Machida, Arisu’s loyal friend. Chota – Played by Yuki Morinaga, Arisu’s friend and companion. Shuntaro Chishiya – Played by Murakami Nijiro, a clever and manipulative strategist. Hatter – Played by Nobuaki Kaneko, leader of a powerful group in Borderland. Kuina – Played by Aya Asahina, a skilled fighter with a complex past.
Themes: Survival under extreme circumstances. Friendship, teamwork, and sacrifice. The value of life and personal growth.
