Official trailer of All of us are Dead

All of Us Are Dead is a South Korean zombie‑apocalypse thriller on Netflix that follows a group of high school students as they struggle to survive a sudden and brutal zombie outbreak at Hyosan High School. The original season became a global hit, known for its intense action, strong emotional bonds, and harrowing survival story. Season 2 continues the story years after the first outbreak, shifting focus from the school to a larger world now under threat as the infection spreads and new dangers emerge. The second season picks up with Nam On‑jo — now a university student in Seoul — who must confront fresh waves of the undead while coping with trauma from her past. As more people are drawn into the chaos, both familiar survivors and new faces are forced into a desperate fight for their lives in an even bigger battle against the expanding zombie menace.

Returning Survivors: Park Ji‑hu as Nam On‑jo — the central character now studying at university, still haunted by past horrors. Yoon Chan‑young as Lee Cheong‑san — a resilient survivor from Season 1. Cho Yi‑hyun as Choi Nam‑ra — the half‑zombie with enhanced abilities who survived the first outbreak. Lomon (Park Solomon) as Lee Su‑hyeok — loyal and brave member of the original group.
